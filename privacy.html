Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

VerbaVox collects the following information to provide our real-time translation services:

  • Phone Number: Used for account verification via SMS. We send a one-time verification code when you sign up or log in.
  • Audio Data: Temporarily processed for real-time translation during calls. Audio is not stored after the call ends.
  • Usage Data: Call duration and feature usage to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity via SMS verification codes
  • To provide real-time voice and video translation services
  • To process payments for subscription services
  • To improve our translation accuracy and app performance

3. SMS Messages

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages from VerbaVox for:

  • Account verification codes (one-time passwords)
  • Security alerts related to your account

Message frequency: Verification codes are sent only when you request them. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt-out.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (Twilio for SMS, Agora for video calls) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Law enforcement when required by law

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of SMS messages by replying STOP

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@verbavox.net

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
