VerbaVox ("we," "us," or "our") is a real-time voice translation mobile application developed and operated by NovaStack LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

VerbaVox collects the following information to provide our real-time translation services:

  • Phone Number: Collected during account registration for identity verification via SMS text message. Your phone number is stored securely and used solely to send one-time verification codes when you request them.
  • Audio Data: Temporarily processed for real-time translation during calls. Audio is processed in real-time and is NOT stored or recorded after the call ends.
  • Usage Data: Call duration, feature usage, and app performance metrics to improve our services.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity via SMS text message verification codes
  • To provide real-time voice and video translation services
  • To process payments for subscription services
  • To improve our translation accuracy and app performance
  • To communicate important service updates

3. SMS/Text Messaging Consent and Terms

Program Name: VerbaVox Account Verification

Program Operator: NovaStack LLC

Your Consent

By providing your mobile phone number and tapping "Send Code" or similar button in the VerbaVox mobile application, you are providing your express written consent to receive SMS text messages from NovaStack LLC (doing business as VerbaVox) at the phone number you provided. You agree that these messages may be sent using an automatic telephone dialing system.

Types of Messages You Will Receive

  • Verification Codes: One-time password (OTP) codes to verify your identity when signing up or logging into your VerbaVox account
  • Security Alerts: Notifications if suspicious activity is detected on your account

We do NOT send marketing, promotional, or advertising messages via SMS.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your use of the app. You will only receive SMS messages when you explicitly request a verification code by initiating the login or signup process. Typical usage is 1-2 messages per authentication attempt. You will not receive recurring automated messages without your action.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please check with your mobile carrier for details about your text messaging plan and any associated costs. We are not responsible for any charges from your carrier.

How to Opt-Out of SMS Messages

You can stop receiving SMS messages from VerbaVox at any time by:

  • Texting STOP to any message you receive from us
  • Emailing support@verbavox.net with the subject "SMS Opt-Out"

After texting STOP, you will receive one final confirmation message stating you have been unsubscribed. After opting out, you will no longer receive SMS messages from VerbaVox. Please note that opting out of SMS messages may limit your ability to use phone-based verification features.

How to Get Help

For help or questions about our SMS program:

  • Text HELP to any message you receive from us
  • Email support@verbavox.net
  • Visit https://verbavox.net

Supported Carriers

Our SMS service is compatible with all major US mobile carriers including but not limited to: AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, U.S. Cellular, and most regional carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Consent Not Required for Purchase

Your consent to receive SMS text messages is NOT required as a condition of purchasing any products or services from VerbaVox. You may use alternative verification methods if available.

Phone Number Privacy

We will not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your phone number will only be used for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and shared only with our SMS service provider (Twilio) for the sole purpose of delivering verification messages to you.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We share limited data with trusted service providers who help us operate our services, under strict confidentiality agreements:

  • Twilio Inc.: SMS text message delivery service - receives your phone number solely to deliver verification codes
  • Agora: Video/voice call infrastructure - no personal data shared
  • Stripe: Payment processing - receives payment information you provide at checkout
  • AI Translation Services: Text/audio for translation - data is anonymized and not linked to your identity

We may also disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) for all data transmission
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Secure authentication practices
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

6. Data Retention

  • Phone Numbers: Retained while your account is active. Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Audio Data: NOT retained. Processed in real-time and immediately discarded after translation.
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 12 months for service improvement, then anonymized or deleted.
  • Payment Information: Stored securely by our payment processor (Stripe) according to their privacy policy.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we have collected
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of SMS messages by replying STOP
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@verbavox.net

If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from your child, please contact us immediately at privacy@verbavox.net and we will delete the information.
