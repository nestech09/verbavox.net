Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

VerbaVox ("we," "us," or "our") is a real-time voice translation mobile application developed and operated by NovaStack LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

VerbaVox collects the following information to provide our real-time translation services:

  • Phone Number: Collected during account registration for identity verification via SMS. Your phone number is used solely to send one-time verification codes.
  • Audio Data: Temporarily processed for real-time translation during calls. Audio is processed in real-time and is NOT stored or recorded after the call ends.
  • Usage Data: Call duration, feature usage, and app performance metrics to improve our services.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity via SMS verification codes
  • To provide real-time voice and video translation services
  • To process payments for subscription services
  • To improve our translation accuracy and app performance
  • To communicate important service updates

3. SMS/Text Messaging Terms

Program Name: VerbaVox Account Verification

What You're Consenting To: By providing your phone number in the VerbaVox app, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from VerbaVox (operated by NovaStack LLC) for account verification purposes.

Types of Messages:

  • One-time verification codes (OTP) when you sign up or log in
  • Security alerts if suspicious activity is detected on your account

Message Frequency: You will only receive SMS messages when you explicitly request a verification code by tapping "Send Code" in the app. Typically 1-2 messages per login attempt. We do NOT send marketing or promotional messages.

Message and Data Rates: Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.

How to Opt-Out: You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message from us
  • Emailing us at support@verbavox.net

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message. Note: Opting out of SMS will prevent you from using phone verification for your account.

Help: For help, reply HELP to any message or email support@verbavox.net

Carriers Supported: Compatible with all major US carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others.

Your Consent is Not Required for Purchase: Consenting to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services from VerbaVox.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share limited data with trusted service providers who help us operate our services:

  • Twilio: SMS delivery for verification codes (receives only your phone number)
  • Agora: Video/voice call infrastructure (no personal data shared)
  • Stripe: Payment processing (receives payment information you provide)
  • AI Translation Services: Text/audio for translation (anonymized, not linked to your identity)

We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure authentication practices
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access to personal data

6. Data Retention

  • Phone Numbers: Retained while your account is active. Deleted upon account deletion request.
  • Audio Data: NOT retained. Processed in real-time and immediately discarded.
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 12 months for service improvement, then anonymized.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of SMS messages by replying STOP
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@verbavox.net

8. Children's Privacy

VerbaVox is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

VerbaVox is operated from the United States. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.
